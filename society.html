my vision for society

  • I believe in a society where we help each other with our skills for free or as much or little as people can give.
  • money is a useful invention as bartering often is not an option and having a universally accepted token for value exchange makes perfect sense, but financial wealth is not the be-all and end-all and should never be a prerequisite for living a happy and independent life.
  • I want to try my best to act as a catalyst for such communities to form and grow.
